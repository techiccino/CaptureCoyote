CaptureCoyote is built for documentation, bug reports, support, tutorials, and follow-up. Capture something quickly, annotate it cleanly, copy or export it, and reopen it later without losing your work.

Why it feels different

Editable screenshots

Save layered .coyote projects and reopen them later with arrows, text, blur, crop, and layout still editable.

Fast annotation workflow

Capture, explain, and finish quickly without the clutter of heavyweight screenshot suites.

Searchable by design

OCR-ready project metadata and searchable snips make screenshots easier to find again later.

Core features

  • Region, window, full-screen, and scrolling capture
  • Arrows, shapes, text, callouts, steps, highlight, and pixelate
  • Editable layered .coyote screenshot projects
  • Copy, quick save, export PNG/JPG, and reopen later
  • OCR review and searchable screenshot metadata
  • Tray launcher and fast post-capture actions
