CaptureCoyote Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026

CaptureCoyote is designed to keep your screenshots and project data on your device. The app does not require an account to use its core features.

What CaptureCoyote stores locally

  • Screenshots and exported images that you choose to save
  • Editable .coyote project files that you choose to create
  • Local settings, recent history, and recovery drafts used to improve your workflow
  • OCR text extracted from screenshots for local search and reopen workflows

What CaptureCoyote does not do

  • It does not require a cloud account
  • It does not include unnecessary telemetry
  • It does not upload your screenshots or project files to a CaptureCoyote server

External links

CaptureCoyote may open external links when you choose actions such as viewing project folders, visiting GitHub, or supporting the project via Ko-fi. Those websites have their own privacy policies and practices.

Your control

You control what you capture, what you save, and what you delete. You can remove project files, exported images, and local app data from your device at any time.

Contact

For project updates and support, visit github.com/techiccino/CaptureCoyote.
